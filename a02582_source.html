11 /*
12  Chains are instances of the
13  model render pipeline
14  You need a different chain for a different size / shape output,
15  or a different thead.
16  When requesting a render of the model,
17  you must use one of its chains.
18 
19  All VideoNode renders happen against a chain.
20  A Chain stores a particular resolution
21  at which the render is to be done.
22  A Chain also stores state that does not belong to any particular VideoNode
23  for the render, i.e. a blank texture and noise texture
24  which is available to all.
25 
26  Chains are immutable once created,
27  that is, you cannot change the size.
28 
29  Chains are created by Outputs or Output-like things
30  (such as the preview adapter.)
31 
32  Chains are thread-safe by nature of being immutable.
33  However, OpenGL thread restrictions still apply
34  (e.g. don't try to access the textures
35  from multiple threads)
36 */
